About

Hola, Holis, Heyy!

My name is Grace Gutierrez and I don't know who is more excited that you are here, you or me. If you landed on my About page it is because you are a tiny bit curious as to how this beautifully intricate brain of mine works and how I got here. I started my career in the Architecture industry as a project manager and designer for luxury residential and commercial projects and transitioned my career towards a more creative world of weddings and events after designing my own wedding.

My passion for authentic expression and innovative approaches at incorporating personal details through luxury experiences is undeniably the thing that drives me. It feels so good getting to see you all enjoy the thing that we've all poured our hearts into on your wedding day. It literally becomes my goal to make sure that you are enjoying every second and know and feel that you are the guest of honor at your own wedding.

I believe that every person deserves to experience what the ultimate attention to detail signifies. Luxury is a mindset and way of life and I believe that on the most special day of your life, you deserve to experience nothing short of that, with of course, a shot of tequila and really good party.
