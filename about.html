About Us

Let Us Bring Your Unique Love Story to Life

I'M SO GLAD YOU'RE HERE.

I'M GRACE

As a first generation American, I was raised in a home that celebrated two different cultures and it is where my foundation of love was established. Spending my childhood in New York and many summers with family in Colombia exposed me to a view of the world that was very different from my peers. It is the very thing that I am so deeply proud of and the reason why I love celebrating moments that honor generations of story-telling with a new perspective.

Telling your story and embracing the details that make you, you will always remain at the core of our process. Having the honor to showcase your love is what fuels our drive to create magic.
