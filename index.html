WE VALUE

AUTHENTICITY

Learning about you is one of our favorite parts of planning your wedding. We are dedicated to understanding what is most important to you. We plan and design weddings that celebrate your individuality by learning about your favorite travel experiences, cultural heritages, and treasured family traditions.

TRANSPARENCY

We believe trust is the foundation of every successful partnership. Feeling comfortable and most importantly, heard, is essential to our open and honest communication. Trusting us gives you the freedom to relax, enjoy yourself, and let us take care of the rest.

ARTISTRY

The heart of your wedding. After we learn what is most important to you, we connect you with trusted and skilled vendors that add to your wedding’s sensory experience. They not only bring amazing skill to your wedding, but also prioritize you and your vision.

CREATIVITY

We draw inspiration from the local surroundings by embracing the beauty and uniqueness of your wedding location and craft a design that feels harmoniously connected.

CONNECTION

Our couples get to truly enjoy their weddings because they go beyond aesthetics, we create experiences where you and your guests feel the magic in the room.

Our Process

Our process is centered around our dedication in helping our couples have a stress-free wedding. Each phase of our planning process focuses on guiding you to make informed decisions that will help bring your vision to life. Our couples can rely on our expertise and lean on our guidance from the very first step all the way till the end of the best day of their lives.

BUDGET DEVELOPMENT

PHASE 1

Our open communication starts at the very beginning with honest discussions about your financial investment and how it will influence your wedding.

LOCATION, VENDORS, & DESIGN

PHASE 2

The look, the flow, the vibes. Choosing a venue and your vendor team is fundamental to our process and will decide the look and feel of your event. Our creative process is focused on choosing the vendors and design that will elevate the space you chose for your wedding.

LOGISTICS

PHASE 3

During phase 3 we focus on all of the extra special details for those extra special moments that will make the difference.

PRODUCTION

Frequently Asked Questions

When in the planning process should we hire you?

The best time to start working with us is at the very beginning of your planning journey. Most couples usually start anywhere between 12 - 15 months before their wedding date.

Do you travel for Events?

Our team is based in New York and Cartagena, Colombia but we love to work with couples who live and want to celebrate anywhere.

Do you offer wedding day(day-of) coordination?

Absolutely, all of our packages include wedding day assistance.

Do you plan other events, such as rehearsal dinners?

Yes! All of our packages are custom tailored to what your needs are for your wedding weekend.
